Early Life and Education

Tony Khan was born on October 10, 1982, in Champaign, Illinois, USA. He is the son of Pakistani-American billionaire Shahid Khan, who is the owner of the Jacksonville Jaguars and Fulham F.C. Tony Khan graduated from the University of Illinois with a degree in finance.

Professional Career

Tony Khan began his professional career working for his father’s company, Flex-N-Gate, before moving on to the sports industry. He played a significant role in the acquisition of the Jacksonville Jaguars and Fulham F.C. by his father.

Role in Jacksonville Jaguars

As the co-owner of the Jacksonville Jaguars, Tony Khan has been instrumental in the team’s operations and management. His innovative strategies and business acumen have contributed to the team’s success and increased its value, thereby adding to his net worth.

Establishment of All Elite Wrestling (AEW)

In 2019, Tony Khan founded All Elite Wrestling (AEW), a professional wrestling promotion company. AEW has quickly risen to prominence, competing with established entities like WWE. The success of AEW has significantly boosted Khan’s net worth.
