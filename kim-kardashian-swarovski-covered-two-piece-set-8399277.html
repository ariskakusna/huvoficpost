Kim Kardashian's Swarovski-Covered Two-Piece Set Is The Best Thing You'll See Today

Kim Kardashian and her shapewear brand SKIMS are a force to be reckoned with. Between partnering with the NBA, WNBA, and USA Basketball as the official underwear of the leagues and launching a nipple bra for a good cause, there’s nothing Kim and SKIMS can’t do. Adding to her list of recent successes? A new SKIMS collaboration with an iconic jewelry brand and its resulting glitzy, glamorous new line.

On Tuesday, the SKIMS mogul was literally shining bright like a diamond at the celebration of her new Swarvoski collaboration with Giovanna Engelbert, the creative director of the brand, which took place during the unveiling of the jewelry brand’s new flagship store on Fifth Avenue. For the occasion, Kim donned a two-piece Swarvoski-covered set comprised of a crystal halter neck top and a coordinating miniskirt layered over a pair of light gray Swarvoski x SKIMS boxers. Letting the crystal-covered intimate shine on its own, Kim accessorized with nothing but a diamond tennis ankle bracelet and a pair of silver and PVC Paris Texas mule sandals.

When it came time for glam, she wore her brunette hair up in a Pamela Anderson-style ‘90s updo and complemented her all-over bronzy glow with feathered lashes, rosy cheeks, and a glossy pink pout.

“For this collaboration, we really wanted to celebrate individual creativity and bring more glamour into getting dressed every day," Kim said about the new line. "The pieces are so dreamy, and we loved integrating the iconic crystal cuts into my favorite SKIMS pieces. We can’t wait for everyone to love Swarvoski x SKIMS as much as I do.’’

Swarvoski x SKIMS is now available at the new Swarovski Flagship Store on Fifth Avenue and online at skims.com.
