Britney Spears‘ new memoir, The Woman In Me, has sold over 1 million copies in the U.S. in its first week, reports AP.

Released on Oct. 24 by Simon & Schuster’s Gallery Books imprint, the celebrity autobiography had already landed on Amazon’s No. 1 Best Sellers book list before it was released. Oscar-nominated actress Michelle Williams narrates the audiobook version of the 288-page tome, which is also available as an e-book for Kindle and other formats. According to Gallery, The Woman In Me has been the company’s fast-selling audio release ever; it runs five hours and 31 minutes and is now No. 1 on Audible’s charts.

“Over the past 15 years or even at the start of my career, I sat back while people spoke about me and told my story for me,” she told People. “After getting out of my conservatorship, I was finally free to tell my story without consequences from the people in charge of my life.”
