Wizkid signs new artiste to his Starboy records

- Wizkid has increased the number of musicians signed to his Starboy record label

- He has signed a little known musician named Terri

- The famous musician made this known via his Instagram page

Nigerian musician Wizkid is well known all over the world for his efforts in taking the Nigerian sound beyond the shores of this country.

In recent times, he has via his record label Starboy given several upcoming musicians the chance at making a name for themselves as well as creating a lasting music career.

On Tuesday, March 28, 2018, he officially signed a little known musician named Terri to his record label. Terri was recently featured on Soco, a Starboy release.

Wizkid took to his Instagram page to share this exciting piece of information writing that: “Young Bull @terri_ryn signed His contract today! Welcome lil bro ! $TARBOY.”

Terri who couldn’t hide his appreciation wrote “Dreams come true!! @wizkidayo forever grateful #Ghetgoals”
