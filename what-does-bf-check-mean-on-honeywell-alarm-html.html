What does bF check mean on Honeywell alarm?

Spread the love

A bF error on a Honeywell Alarm Keypad indicates that there is an issue with the alarm monitoring communicator for your panel, according to Honeywell. If the communicator has been disabled, this indicates that it is not communicating effectively. bF stands for “backup failure” in the error message.
