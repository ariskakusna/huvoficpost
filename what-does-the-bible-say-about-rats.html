What does it mean if you see a rat in the day?

Seeing rats during the day is considered to be a sign of an infestation with great scale. In fact, this is more of a myth than an actual fact. Rats are nocturnal animals, which means they are more active during the night, however, seeing them during daylight is something completely normal.

    • What is the difference between mice and rats?

    Rats and mice are both rodents, so look similar - the biggest difference is their size. Rats are larger and heavier while mice have smaller slender bodies. Mice also have long slender tails (for their body size) covered in hair compared to rat tails which are shorter, thicker and hairless.
