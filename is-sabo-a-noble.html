Is Sabo a noble?

Despite being the son of a noble, Sabo does not act like a stereotypical noble would act, nor does he consider himself to be a noble. He does not look down on the less fortunate nor does he put on airs about his status.

Is Sabo a noble in One Piece?

Sabo is actually of noble descent, as he is the son of Outlook III, as well as the adopted brother of Stelly and the current king of the Goa Kingdom. Sabo had befriended Ace at a very young age after escaping the clutches of his noble family, with him later meeting Luffy as well.

Who was Sabo trained by?

After joining the Revolutionary Army, Sabo was personally trained by Monkey D. Dragon. By the time he was 22, he achieved the rank of Chief of Staff, and thus the second in command, of the Revolutionary Army.
