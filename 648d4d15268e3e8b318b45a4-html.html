Tekashi 6ix9ine dating Yailin La Mas Viral? $500K in gifts hint at new romance

Tekashi 6ix9ine recently gave reggaeton artist Yailin La Mas Viral a series of gifts with an estimated worth of $500,000 after they released their first song together.

A set of suggestive images and gifts from rapper Tekashi 6ix9ine to Yailin La Más Viral went viral on social media and entertainment shows after the rapper showered the Dominican reggaeton artist following their collaboration while he preps his audience for his reggaeton album.

Tekashi 6ix9ine monetizes controversy

The single is called "Pa ti" and has taken off in digital platforms amid strong rumors that the American rapper and the Quisqueya singer are already dating, something that Anuel AA must not be so happy about, because besides being friends with Tekashi, he just separated from the interpreter of "Chivirika" and worse when she was about to give birth to Cattleya, their daughter.

Gifts worth more than half a million

In the video that both reggaeton artists shared on social media, Tekashi showered Yailin with gifts, including a handbag and a luxurious diamond-encrusted watch whose cost, some say, could exceed $500,000 dollars.

In addition, sources quoted by many media claim that Tekashi and Yailin have already been spotted together in the streets of Santo Domingo.

Meanwhile, Tekashi 6ix9ine has released a full reggaeton album completely in Spanish called 'Leyenda Viva' meaning Living Legend.
