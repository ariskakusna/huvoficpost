Eva Mendes reacts to Ryan Gosling in candid behind-the-scenes moments

The Barbie star revisited his Drive era for the newest Tag Heuer commercial

    Ryan Gosling has been taking the world by storm once again since his first debut as Ken for the upcoming Barbie movie, but took things to a grungier level for his latest appearance.

    The actor starred in the latest Tag Heuer commercial titled "The Chase for Carrera," in which he revisited his darker style and fast-paced attitude akin to the kind he explored in 2011's .

    Starring alongside star Vanessa Bayer, it features Ryan playing himself as he drives off with one of the luxury brand's premium pieces as Vanessa (playing "The Prop Master") gives chase.

    Following a star-studded premiere, the spot quickly went viral and caught the attention of none other than Ryan's doting partner of 12 years, Eva Mendes.

    The 49-year-old took to Instagram with a clip from the commercial that featured several behind-the-scenes bloopers, many of which primarily saw Ryan messing up his lines or goofing around on set.

    The scenes provided for a more candid look at the usually suave star's sillier side, and Eva couldn't stop herself from gushing over them.
