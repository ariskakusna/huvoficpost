Jennifer Lopez Wears Lime Green Houndstooth Coat to Go Christmas Tree Shopping

Jennifer Lopez looked so festive while Christmas tree shopping with her blended family.

The Marry Me star and husband Ben Affleck took their kids to pick out the perfect spruce in Los Angeles over the weekend.

For the outing, Lopez wore a long tweed houndstooth trench coat by Chanel in lime green and white. It was embellished with golden buttons with the French fashion house's logo. The multi-hyphenate let the unexpected, gorgeous piece do the talking, pairing it simply with a plain white T-shirt, blue wide-leg jeans, and white sneakers. She accessorized with a white Dior belt with gold hardware, and a gold necklace with a hummingbird pendant.

Affleck kept it casual but stylish for the outing. The Deep Water star wore a tan hoodie, dark blue jeans, and a pair of multicolored Nike sneakers.

The kids stayed warm and comfortable in relaxed sweatshirts and sneakers.

In photos, Affleck and Lopez are seen walking happily with their children: Lopez's twins with Marc Anthony, Max and Emme; and Affleck's three kids with Jennifer Garner, Violet, Seraphina, and Samuel.

This coming Christmas will be Affleck and Lopez's first as a married couple and with their blended family. The pair, who rekindled their romance after two decades just last year, married in a secret Las Vegas ceremony this July. The low-key nuptials were attended only by the couple's kids. Then, one month later, the actors held an official, grand ceremony with their families and loved ones at Affleck's estate in Riceboro, Georgia.
