Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story Release Date and Episode Count

If you are a fan of Bridgerton, you must have heard of its spin-off Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story, which is soon arriving on Netflix. The new series will focus on the fan-favorite character and showcase the young Queen’s rise to power and prominence.

Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story will give the fans some sort of relief who have been patiently waiting for Bridgerton season 3 for over a year. So when will the spin-off series be released on Netflix and how many episodes will it feature? Read on to find out.

Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story: Release Date and Episode Count

Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story will feature six episodes, which is two less than its predecessor series. Audiences would not have to wait for new episodes every week as the whole season will be dropped at once on Netflix on Thursday, May 4th, 2023. Here is the full list of episodes:

  • Episode 1: Queen to Be
  • Episode 2: Honeymoon Bliss
  • Episode 3: Even Days
  • Episode 4: Holding the King
  • Episode 5: Gardens in Bloom
  • Episode 6: Crown Jewels

Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story: Plot and Trailer

The show will center around Queen Charlotte’s marriage to King George, including their love story and the Queen’s eventual rise to power. Showrunner Shonda Rhimes talked about the theme of the show in a recent interview and said. “It’s very much about how do you survive in a world in which you have no power. Where do you find the power in that?”

“When I think about this series and what I want audiences to take away from it, I think the most important thing is I want people to feel like this is what happens with real love, love is hard love is difficult, love has many layers, and I also want them to take away the idea that the happy ending that we always talk about for characters doesn’t have to be the obvious one,” she added. You can check out the trailer for the series below.

The official synopsis by Netflix reads, “Centered on Queen Charlotte’s rise to prominence and power, this Bridgerton-verse prequel tells the story of how the young Queen’s marriage to King George sparked both a great love story and a societal shift, creating the world of the Ton inherited by the characters in Bridgerton.”

Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story: Cast

Golda Rosheuvel, who plays Queen Charlotte in Bridgerton, will reprise the role in the spin-off. The younger version of the queen will be played by India Amarteifio. The cast also includes Adjoa Andoh as Lady Agatha Danbury, Arsema Thomas as younger Agatha Danbury, Ruth Gemmell as Violet, Dowager Countess Bridgerton and James Fleet as King George III.

Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story also stars Corey Mylchreest as younger King George IIIMichelle Fairley as Dowager Princess Augusta, Hugh Sachs as Brimsley, Sam Clemmett as younger Brimsley, Richard Cunningham as Lord Bute, Tunji Kasim as Adolphus, Rob Maloney as the Royal Doctor and Cyril Nri as Lord Danbury.
