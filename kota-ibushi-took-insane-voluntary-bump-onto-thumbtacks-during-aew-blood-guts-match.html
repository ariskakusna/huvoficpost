Kota Ibushi Took Insane Voluntary Bump Onto Thumbtacks During AEW Blood &amp; Guts Match

AEW presented another Blood & Guts match, and it was quite a show. In the end, The Elite won, and Kota Ibushi made quite a debut with AEW.

The Blood & Guts match is usually full of unexpected, and often gruesome moments. This year was no different, because there was broken glass, nail covered boards, and thumbtacks galore.

Kota Ibushi was really excited about getting involved in his first Blood & Guts match. A fan at ringside captured a video of Kota Ibushi eyeing a pile of thumbtacks, before he did something quite insane.

It appears as if Kota Ibushi rubbed a handful of tacks on his chest before he took a flat back bump on his own onto the pile of tacks. What resulted next was Kota Ibushi getting up and strutting around to show off his new collection of tacks on his back.

Hangman Page can be seen in the short video, as he caught a glimpse of Kota Ibushi’s back. It appeared that Page had a smile on his face as he took in the unpredictable act.

We will have more coverage of AEW, right here at Ringside News. Blood & Guts was a big night for the company, but even bigger things could be coming down the pipeline.
