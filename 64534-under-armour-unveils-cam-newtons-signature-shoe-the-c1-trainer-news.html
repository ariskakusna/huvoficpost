Under Armour Unveils Cam Newton's Signature Shoe, The C1N Trainer

Cam Newton's Under Armour signature sneaker will be hitting retailers next week and today we have our first official look at the brand new Under Armour C1N.

The C1N trainer, Cam's first ever off-field signature shoe, will make it's debut in an all-red "Hometown" colorway, a nod to Cam's Atlanta roots, featuring gold detailing on the lace tips, UA branding on the heel, and the eyelet which is Cam's "C1N" logo.
