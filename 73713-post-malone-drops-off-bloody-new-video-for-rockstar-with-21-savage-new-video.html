Post Malone Drops Off Bloody New Video For Rockstar With 21 Savage

After hitting us with an unofficial video a couple weeks ago, Post Malone decides to come through today and finally share the official video for his new chart-topping single “Rockstar” with 21 Savage, which he had been teasing all day on Instagram. The song, which is already platinum, is currently enjoying its 5th week at No. 1 on Billboard’s Hot 100 chart.

Opening up with a parental advisory warning, Post delivers a gory blood bath of a visual that features some Samauri sword fights and plenty of dead bodies. Directed by Jake Nava, Post and a sexy female accomplice fight for their lives in this slow moving, yet action-packed short film (above). It looks like something out of the movie Kill Bill to say the least.

Check out the official video (above) and let us know what you think. Single available now on iTunes.
