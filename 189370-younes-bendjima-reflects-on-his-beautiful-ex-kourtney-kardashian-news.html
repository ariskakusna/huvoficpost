Younes Bendjima Reflects On His &quot;Beautiful&quot; Ex Kourtney Kardashian

With minutes left within the year, a lot of people can't help but take a walk down memory lane of great moments in 2018. The latest person to do it is Younes Bendjima who briefly dated mom of three Kourtney Kardashian. The model shared a post on his Instagram story from when the former couple took a romantic trip to a sunset/ sunrise paradise. 

“Let’s not forget about this beautiful woman inside and out, I’m not the type of man that forget moments like that,” he wrote on his Instagram story. [You're] an amazing woman and mum and I hope you will find happiness for you and your children it’s all love at the end of the day we don’t know what can happen tmw so there you go.”

Kourtney and Younes split in August for unknown reasons, but after the media started alluding at possible issues and reporting he had moved on with Tyga's ex, Younes made it clear that he wasn't having it.

"Once again you guys failed," he wrote. "I'm not attached to the "life" so you can't touch me. I know who I am where I'm from and where I'm going and that bothers you. Only one opinion matter The one of my Lord."
