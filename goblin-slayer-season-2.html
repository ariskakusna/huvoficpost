Goblin Slayer Season 2 Spoiler, English Dub Release Date, Plotlines, Cast, Episode &amp; What to Expect

Goblin Slayer Season 2:- Goblin Slayer is a dark fantasy anime series that follows the adventures of a ruthless warrior who dedicates his life to exterminating goblins, the most vile and dangerous creatures in the world. The anime is based on a light novel series of the same name by Kumo Kagyu and Noboru Kannatsuki, which has also spawned a manga adaptation and a prequel spin-off. The first season of the anime aired in 2018 and received positive reviews from fans and critics alike, who praised its gritty and realistic portrayal of goblin slaying, as well as its compelling characters and story.

After a long wait, the second season of Goblin Slayer is finally here, and fans are eager to see more of the goblin slayer and his party of adventurers, who face new threats and challenges in their quest to protect humanity from the goblin menace. In this article, we will provide you with everything you need to know about Goblin Slayer Season 2, including its release date, time zone, countdown, spoiler, storyline, plot, ending explanation, recap of season 1, ratings, expectations, cast and characters, episodes, trailer, and where to watch.

Goblin Slayer Season 2 Release Date

Goblin Slayer Season 2 premiered on October 6, 2023, in Japan, and is scheduled to run for 12 episodes, ending on December 22, 2023. The anime is produced by studio Liden Films, which also worked on the first season, and is directed by Takaharu Ozaki, who returns from the first season as well. The series composer is Hideyuki Kurata, who also wrote the script for the anime adaptation of the Goblin Slayer: Goblin’s Crown movie, which served as a bridge between the first and second seasons.

The anime is available for streaming on Crunchyroll, which simulcasts the original Japanese version with English subtitles, as well as other languages such as Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Italian, Russian, and Arabic. Crunchyroll also offers an English dub for the anime, which premiered on October 20, 2023, two weeks after the original version. The English dub is expected to come out every Friday on Crunchyroll in the US and other select regions.

Storyline and Plot

Goblin Slayer Season 2 continues the storyline and plot of the first season, which follows the goblin slayer, a silver-ranked adventurer who has devoted his life to killing goblins, the most common and hated monsters in the world. He is joined by a party of fellow adventurers, who share his goal of protecting humanity from the goblin menace. They are:

  • Priestess: A young and naive cleric who is the first to join the goblin slayer, after he saves her from a goblin attack that killed her original party. She is loyal and supportive of the goblin slayer and often acts as his moral compass. She uses miracles to heal and protect her allies, as well as to harm her enemies.
  • High Elf Archer: A cheerful and energetic elf who is an expert archer and a member of an ancient elven clan. She joins the goblin slayer out of curiosity and boredom, and also to prove herself as an adventurer. She is often annoyed by the goblin slayer’s methods and attitude but also respects him for his skills and determination. She uses a longbow and arrows to snipe her foes from a distance, as well as a dagger for close combat.
  • Dwarf Shaman: A wise and sarcastic dwarf who is a master of earth magic and alchemy. He joins the goblin slayer to seek new challenges and experiences and also to keep an eye on the high elf archer, who is his old friend and rival. He often bickers with the high elf archer over their racial differences but also cares for her as a comrade.
