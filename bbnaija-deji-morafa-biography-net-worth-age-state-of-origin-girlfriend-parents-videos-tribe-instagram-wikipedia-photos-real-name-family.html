Biography

Ayodeji “Deji” Morafa (born May 25, 1995) is a talented Nigerian pharmacist, digital creator, and fragrance enthusiast. 

He works as a Quality Assurance Specialist at AIM Immuno Tech. He has worked as an Epidemiologist, Data Processing Specialist, and Pharmacy Technician at reputable companies in the United States. 

BBNaija Deji Morafa became an international star after being unveiled as a housemate in the 7th Big Brother Naija show season.

Early Life

BBNaija Deji Morafa was born to the illustrious Morafa family on the 25th of May 1995. He was named Ayodeji Morafa by his parents. He was born and raised in Lagos City. He was born to Mr. & Mrs. Morafa.

Deji Morafa is a Yoruba man and is an indigene of Lagos State. He comes from a Christian family of four. He has a younger brother named Oluwaseun Ayodeji. He speaks English and Yoruba fluently.

BBNaija Deji Morafa is a gifted child who excelled academically in elementary and secondary school. From his childhood, he aspired to work in the health care sector. This dream influenced his decision to study pharmacy at the university. 

Education

BBNaija Deji Morafa underwent primary and secondary education at leading private-owned schools in Lagos City. 

After graduating from high school, he gained admission into the University Of Lagos (UNILAG) in 2011. He bagged a degree in Pharmacy from UNILAG in 2016.

He has a Master’s degree in Health Informatics specializing in Data Analytics. He bagged a second degree in 2022 from Logan University. 

Career

After graduating from the University of Lagos, BBNaija Deji Morafa observed the mandatory one-year NYSC program, where he kickstarted his work experience in the pharmacy field. 

Thereafter, he gained employment at Lagos University Teaching Hospital (LUTH) and worked at the health institution for some years. Afterward, he migrated overseas in search of better work opportunities. 

Deji Morafa took up residence in the United States of America and started his work career in the country as a Pharmacy Technician at Capsule in January 2020. He held this role for four months before he was promoted to Inventory Specialist. Three months later, he was promoted for the second time at Capsule to the Data Entry & Processing Specialist position. 

In January 2021, he secured a contract role as an Epidemiologist at New York City Health and Hospitals Corporation. He worked with the organization for seven months. Since July 2021, he has worked as a Quality Assurance Specialist at AIM Immuno Tech, one of the leading health companies in the United States. 

In a twist event, BBNaija Deji Morafa was announced as a housemate on the Big Brother Naija reality show on the 31st of July 2022. The news came one week after the 7th season of the show began officially. Together with Modella, Deji Morafa is one of the two additional housemates that joined the show to compete for its grand prize of 100 million naira.
