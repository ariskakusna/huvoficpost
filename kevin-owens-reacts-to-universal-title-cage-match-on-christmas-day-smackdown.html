Kevin Owens Reacts To Universal Title Cage Match On Christmas Day SmackDown

Kevin Owens took a hellacious beating at WWE TLC. Roman Reigns defeated Owens, but he also used Jey Uso’s help throughout the match. This week on SmackDown, a steel cage will greatly hinder that possibility.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE TLC 2020.

WWE announced that Kevin Owens will face Roman Reigns in a cage match on Christmas Day. That episode will actually be taped tomorrow, but it will air on Friday.

Owens tweeted out in response to this Universal Title match. He refuses to stay down because he simply can’t do it.

Can’t stay down.

Won’t stay down.

Roman Reigns is okay after that brutal TLC match. He was just selling his shoulder, but he has another war in store for him tomorrow as he will meet Kevin Owens in a steel cage.

They have also announced that Sami Zayn will defend the IC Title against Big E. There are two title matches in store for Friday Night SmackDown on Christmas Day. We will have to wait and see if spoilers leak, from the December 22nd ThunderDome tapings, prior to the event airing.
