The 25+ Best Hunting Movies, Ranked By Hunters

A thrilling tale set in colonial East Africa, this 1996 film is based on the true story of two man-eating lions which terrorized workers during the construction of the Uganda Railway. British engineer John Patterson, played by Val Kilmer, teams up with legendary hunter Charles Remington (Michael Douglas) in a desperate attempt to hunt down the elusive and deadly beasts. The story masterfully blends themes of fear, determination, and the quest for dominance between man and nature, as the hunters find themselves becoming the hunted.
