Kevin Tsujihara: biography, family, career

The businessman was born Kevin Ken Tsujihara on October 25, 1964 , making him 56 years old in 2020. He was born and raised as the son of Shizuo and Miyeko “Mickey” Tsujihara in Petaluma, California, USA. He has the ethnicity Japanese American and her parents owned the Empire Egg company.

Regarding his education, after finishing high school, he entered the University of Southern California. From college, he earned his bachelor’s degree and then went on to earn an MBA from Stanford Graduate of Southern California. After graduation, he tried to go into business and opened a company called QuickTax Inc. However, his tax preparation website company failed and he moved to Los Angeles in search of a better opportunity.

In 1994, he joined Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc as chief financial officer for special projects. He initially handled the company’s interest in Six Flags theme parks in 1990. After that, he won promotion to president of Warner Bros. in 2005. He then became the company’s new CEO when Barry Meyer announced his retirement. from the post on January 1, 2013. Thus, he became the first Asian-American to become the CEO of a major Hollywood studio in the 90-year history of Warner Bros.
