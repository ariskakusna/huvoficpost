Beyonc Wears a Skintight, PVC Bodysuit from Ivy Park x Adidas

When the new Ivy Park x Adidas collection finally drops this month, it's likely to sell out seriously fast. One of the reasons the new collection, which is dubbed "Icy Park," won't be in stock for long is because Beyoncé herself looks so incredible in every single item.

In a series of new photos shared on Instagram this weekend, Beyoncé models a skintight bodysuit, worn simply with a pair of sneakers and a selection of gold jewelry. The low-cut, high-rise bodysuit appears to be made of PVC, which glistens on the superstar. If you've been searching for an item which will perfectly display your curves, this might be the piece of clothing you've been after, and as usual, Beyoncé looks flawless.

Queen Bey has been promoting the forthcoming Ivy Park x Adidas rollout in recent weeks, and has even enlisted the help of Hailey Bieber and Gucci Mane to do so. The new items will be available online from February 19, and will drop in Adidas stores on February 20.

The superstar's Ivy Park line was previously co-owned by British retailer Topshop, but Beyoncé decided to buy back her brand in 2018. In a statement regarding her new collaboration with Adidas, the "Halo" singer said, "Adidas has had tremendous success in pushing creative boundaries. We share a philosophy that puts creativity, growth and social responsibility at the forefront of business. I look forward to re-launching and expanding Ivy Park on a truly global scale with a proven, dynamic leader."
