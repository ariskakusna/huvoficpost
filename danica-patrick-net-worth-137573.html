Danica Patrick Net Worth 2024

Danica Patrick is a professional driver born on March 25, 1982 in Beloit, WI. She made history in 2005 when she was named the Indianapolis 500 and IndyCar Series Rookie of the Year. In 2008, she became the first female driver to win an IndyCar Series race after coming in first at the Indy Japan 300.
