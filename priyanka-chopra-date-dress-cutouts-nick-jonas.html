Priyanka Chopra's Sultry Date-Night Dress Is Covered in Cutouts

At a recent Jonas Brothers concert at Los Angeles’s Dodger Stadium, Priyanka Chopra supported her husband in a showstopping dress covered in cutouts.

Nick Jonas shared black-and-white pictures of the night on Twitter, including snaps of himself performing onstage and a PDA-filled shot in which he’s embracing Chopra.

For the outing, the Citadel star looked glamorous in a strappy dress with large cutouts across the chest. The fabric at the front was held together by metallic rings with a spherical bulb attached at the middle. She accessorized sparingly, opting to wear a delicate chain necklace with a tiny pendant.

Meanwhile, the singer repped L.A.’s baseball team in a Dodgers T-shirt.

“From seeing games with my brothers, to one of my first dates with @priyankachopra and now playing a show at Dodger Stadium,” Jonas captioned the photos. “What a full circle moment.”

Last month, the couple celebrated the kickoff of the Jonas Brothers World Tour in New York City by sharing some sweet backstage pictures.

At the time, Chopra wore a bedazzled black-and-white checkered crop top by Nué paired with a long black column skirt, a cropped black leather jacket, a silver Bulgari handbag, and more Bulgari jewelry.

Jonas stood out in an all-white outfit consisting of a loose button-up, a T-shirt, and high-waist pleated trousers. For a pop of color, he added red-tinted sunglasses.

“You are a magnet @nickjonas MM and I are so lucky to have you❤️,” she wrote on Instagram at the time. “Congratulations on the start of an incredible tour. You’re all in for a huge ride! Let’s gooooo! Great job JB team the Band, the crew. The show was seamless and awe inspiring. Round 2 tonight! ❤️🙏🏽👏💪🏽.”
