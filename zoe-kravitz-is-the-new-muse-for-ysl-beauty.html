Zoe Kravitz has landed a major beauty campaign

    Zoe Kravitz has landed one of her biggest campaigns to date. The 27-year-old star has been revealed as YSL Beauty's newest muse, in a social media collaboration which will see the actress sharing her favourite make-up, beauty looks and tips with fans across the brand's social media channels, as well as her own.

    Zoe Kravitz is the new muse of YSL Beauty

    "YSL has always been one of my favourite brands. Their ability to combine the chicest of style with a raw edge has always been to me one of the best ways to express yourself through fashion and beauty," Zoe explained in a statement released by the brand. "Nothing is forced, nothing is fake, but everything is bold, strong, and unafraid. I'm very excited to work with this legendary and timeless company."

    Theactress will also feature in a short video from the fashion label titled , although further details of the project have yet to be revealed.

    Zoe turned heads at the Met Gala on Monday night

    Zoe, who is the daughter of Lenny Kravitz and Lisa Bonet, joins an already impressive line-up of YSL Beauty brand ambassadors with supermodels including Coco Rocha, Jourdan Dunn and Cara Delevingne having all fronted campaigns for the cosmetics giant.

    Theactress recently showed off her impressive beauty credentials when she headed to the Met Gala, turning heads with bold eye make-up, pink lipstick and a dramatic black lace mask, complementing her striking black haute couture minidress.

    The star will be sharing her favourite beauty products and tips as part of the campaign

    MET GALA 2016: THE SHOWSTOPPING BEAUTY LOOKS

    Ahead of the glamorous bash, Zoe teamed up with YSL beauty to get ready, with the brand sharing sneak peeks of the process on their Instagram account. Zoe looked every inch a beauty icon as she sat in the hair and make-up chair in a fluffy white dressing gown, sipping on champagne in between getting her beauty look complete. Her glossy black tresses were styled into 1920s-inspired finger waves, while her make-up was kept simple, with pale pink lipstick and chic black winged eyeliner.
