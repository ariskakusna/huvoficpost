Cynthia Rowley's Wrapping Paper Almost Makes What's Inside Not Matter

Everyone has that perfectly put-together friend who gives expertly wrapped giftswith adorable bows and the sort of chic paper most may not even know where or how to find. Cynthia Rowley is making it easier for you to become one of those friends. The multi-talented designer's Artist Editions Wrapping Paper is inspired by flowers, which she's abstracted into what she calls, "a flash mob of flora and animated shapes." The variety of three designs retail for $32.00 for 15 sheets and is available exclusively on cynthiarowley.com and home flash sale site onekingslane.com tomorrow, December 7. Consider it one small step on your path to perfection.
