Karim Benzema signs 1 year contract extension at Spanish side Real Madrid

Karim Benzema has signed a new one year contract deal at the Bernabeu which will make him remain at the club until the end of 2021/22 League season.

Karim Benzema has been playing for Real Madrid for the past 10 years and has featured in close to 500 games scoring 238 goals for Los Blancos.

He was recently linked with a January 2020 move to Premier League side Arsenal, but as it stands now, the Gunners will have to look elsewhere for a striker.

Already this season, Karim Benzema has netted 16 goals in 23 games in all competitions and he has been consistent in front of goals for Real Madrid.

According to the report by Marca, Karim Benzema's superb relationship with Real Madrid boss Florentino Perez was key to him signing a new deal.
