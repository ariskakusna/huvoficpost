Watch Lil Wayne's Full &quot;Nightline&quot; Interview

When asked about Black Lives Matter, Wayne responded, "What is it? What do you mean? That just sounds weird. I don’t know, that you put a name on it…It’s not a name, it’s not, ‘whatever whatever.’ It’s somebody got shot by a policeman for a fucked up reason.”

Elsewhere in the interview, he pulls out a red flag from his pocket and proclaims, "I'm connected to this motherfucking flag. I'm a gang banger, man. I'm connected."

Host Lindsey Davis said that the interview ended "angrily." Wayne removed his mic and said, ""I ain't no motherfucking politician."
