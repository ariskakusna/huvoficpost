Aug 4, 2024 Image courtesy of Fasque Castle Accommodation Set in the middle of private forested parkland in north-east Scotland, this Victorian castle – an exclusive-use venue – is one of Scotland's grandest and most historically significant houses. In the 19th century it was the family home of the four-time British Prime MinisterWilliam Gladstone, and was considered the most sophisticated building in the country at the time.
