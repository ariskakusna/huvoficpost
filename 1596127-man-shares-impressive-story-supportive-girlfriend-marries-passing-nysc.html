Man Shares Impressive Story of His Supportive Girlfriend, Marries Her After Passing Out From NYSC

  • A man shared the story of how he met his wife on Facebook when he was still in school and had little or nothing
  • The man said his girlfriend supported him and stood by him until he completed his education and went for NYSC

A Nigerian man is happy that he has finally married the woman who stood by him in critical times.

He noted that he was selling moi moi in school so as to be able to pay his school fees.

MC noted that he would hawk moi moi early in the morning before hurrying off to attend classes.
