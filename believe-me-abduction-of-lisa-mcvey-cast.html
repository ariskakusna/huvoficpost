Who in the cast of Believe Me: The Abduction of Lisa McVey?

Katie Douglas as Lisa McVey

Lisa McVey, who was abducted when she was 17, is played in the film by Katie Douglas.

In 2021 the 22-year-old Canadian actress played Ginny’s friend Abby in Ginny And Georgia on Netflix.

Katie has also been in Spooksville and Mary Kills People.

Rossif Sutherland as Bobby Joe Long

Evil serial killer Bobby Joe Long is played by actor Rossif Sutherland.

He’s known for being in River, Guest Of Honour, Hyena Road, Reign and High Life.

Rossif comes from Hollywood royalty as his dad is Donald Sutherland and his brother is Keifer.

David James Elliott as Sgt Larry Pinkerton

The detective who believes Lisa's story is Sergeant Larry Pinkerton.

He is played by Canadian actor David James Elliott.

The star is best known as lead character, Harmon Rabb Jr, in JAG.

He also starred in 2020 Netflix drama Spinning Out.

Megan Fahlenbock as Betty

Megan Fahlenbock plays Lisa’s mother Betty.

The Canadian actress has worked in animation and has also had a few film roles.

Megan is best known for her voice roles as Gwen on Total Drama, Jen Masterson on 6teen, and Eva Kant in Diabolik.
