Patty Hearst Net Worth

Patty Hearst was born on February 20, 1954 in San Francisco, CA. She was the granddaughter of newspaper pioneer William Randolph Hearst. In 1974, she was kidnapped by the Symbionese Liberation Army (SLA) and forced to participate in their criminal activities, including murders and robberies. After her release, she went on to have a successful acting career, appearing in TV series such as Veronica Mars, Boston Common, and Frasier.
