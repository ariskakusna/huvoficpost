Wimbledon 2014: celebrities in attendance so far

    Wimbledon 2014 is underway and the celebrities already have been out in force to watch the first games of the championship - including cheering on reigning Wimbledon champion Andy Murray who won his first game.

    Andy Murray's game on Monday was attended by long-time partner Kim Sears, while comedians Jack Whitehall and John Bishop were also snapped in the stands, as British actress and former Bond girl Naomie Harris watched the proceedings with her children.

    Tess Daly and husband Vernon Kay sat behind Tess's formerco-host Sir Bruce Forsyth on Tuesday as they watched the Julia Glushko v Sabine Lisicki match on centre court.

    Tess looked Wimbledon-ready in her white short-sleeved dress, while Vernon and Bruce wore smart suits.

    The Duchess of Cornwall was at Wimbledon on Wednesday

    Hugh Jackman showed off his recently-shaven head and growing mustache at the same match.

    The Australian actor is currently in the UK filming his latest movie, , and at one point caught the attention of Marion Bartoli, who won the 2013 Women's championship before retiring from the sport.

    ​Television presenter Jonathon Ross,judge Alesha Dixon and British actresses Lily James and Ophelia Lovibond all watched the action of the Martin Klizan v Rafael Nadal game on Tuesday.

    Meanwhile the Duchess of Cornwall was seen on Wednesday as she met Laura Robson at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club.
