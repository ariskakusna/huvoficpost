Will Ospreay Confirms AEW Deal Includes Provision for NJPW Return

Will Ospreay is one of the greatest high-flying wrestlers in the world today. He is known for his jaw-dropping stunts in the ring.

Ospreay made a name for himself in New Japan Pro Wrestling where he quickly became the company’s best bout machine due to his tenacity to put on incredible matches. Sadly, Ospreay’s tenure with the company ended in NJPW New Beginning in Osaka where he competed in his final NJPW match.

Now that Ospreay has completed his obligations with NJPW, he will move on to AEW where he has signed a deal. However, Ospreay addressed the media following his final match and said that his AEW deal allows him to return to NJPW.

“I signed my deal with AEW, and I’m allowed to come back and do New Japan matches.” 

“I don’t know when that’s going to be. I need to find balance first because I have no idea what I’m doing right now.” 

“I’m scared for this next job. I’ve had anxiety traveling to America. It’s not that I don’t enjoy it, I just get scared. I’ve always done this on my own,”

“I promise you guys on fucking everything, I will be back. I promise you. I will be back.”

This announcement from Will Ospreay will make NJPW fans happy as they will get to witness the aerial assassin compete in the ring again. Stay tuned to Ringside News as we stay on this story.
