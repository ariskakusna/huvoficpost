Yvonne Nelson Sells More Copies Of Her Book And Gets Mobbed By Excited Fans

  • Yvonne Nelson's memoir remains one of Ghana's top-selling books weeks after its release
  • The memoir, which focuses on her life experiences and the search for her biological father, has created massive controversies
  • While signing copies of the book to her ardent followers at the University of Ghana Bookshop, she was greeted by a massive crowd who were ready to grab copies

Ghanaian actress and movie producer Yvonne Nelson has got her books selling rapidly after its release. The actress was mobbed and greeted by an excited crowd as she stormed the University of Ghana Bookshop to sell and sign some copies.

In a remarkable display of admiration and support, Yvonne Nelson was greeted by an exuberant crowd of students at the University of Ghana Bookshop as she visited to sign copies of her top-selling memoir, I Am Not Yvonne Nelson.
