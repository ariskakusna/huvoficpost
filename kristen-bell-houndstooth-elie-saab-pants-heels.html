Kristen Bell channels office-chic in designer pants and heels

The actress looked timeless in monochrome tones

    Kristen Bell is beloved for her extroverted personality - so it comes as no surprise that even when she dons a paired-down look, she still makes a splash. The star sported a monochrome ensemble during a press tour for her latest movie and she served up office-chic for the occasion.

    The 42-year-old looked mesmerizing but modest in a classic black round-neck knit bodysuit which she paired with some beautiful Elie Saab houndstooth pants. Featuring a high-waisted silhouette and a black and white print, the pants looked divine when teamed with some timeless black point-toe heels.
