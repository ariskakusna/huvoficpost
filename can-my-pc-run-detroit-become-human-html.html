Will Your PC Become Human? (Or Just Run Detroit: Become Human)

Ah, Detroit: Become Human. A game that'll have you questioning your own humanity (or lack thereof) as you navigate a world where androids are more real than ever. But before you dive into this existential crisis simulator, there's a crucial question: can your trusty PC handle it?

Fear not, fellow gamer! We're here to crack open that question and see if your PC is ready to become...well, not sentient, but Detroit: Become Human ready.

The Minimum You Need (Robot Uprising Not Included)

Detroit: Become Human isn't asking for the moon. Here's the bare minimum your PC needs to become a playable machine (don't worry, it won't become self-aware...probably):

  • CPU: Intel Core i5-2300 @ 2.8 GHz or its AMD/alien overlord equivalent (AMD Ryzen 3 1200 @ 3.1GHz or AMD FX-8350 @ 4.2GHz)
  • RAM: 8 GB of RAM (though 16 GB is recommended for smoother gameplay)
  • Graphics Card: Nvidia GeForce GTX 780 or AMD HD 7950 with at least 3GB of VRAM. (Remember, folks, Vulkan 1.1 support is mandatory!)
  • Operating System: Gotta be Windows 10 (64-bit) for this one. Sorry Linux penguins and Mac enthusiasts, this time it's Windows or bust.
  • Storage: Make sure you have a spare 55 GB lying around for the game itself.

Basically, if you built your PC in the last decade, you're probably good to go.

The Recommended Specs (For a Truly Glorious Future)

Now, if you want to experience Detroit: Become Human in all its graphical glory (because let's face it, those androids are easy on the eyes), then you'll need to step up your game (pun intended):

  • CPU: Intel Core i5-6600 @ 3.3 GHz or AMD Ryzen 3 1300X @ 3.4 GHz
  • RAM: 12 GB of RAM is your sweet spot here.
  • Graphics Card: Nvidia GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 580 with at least 4GB of VRAM.

With these specs, you'll be able to crank up the settings and witness the beauty of a world on the brink of robot revolution...in stunning detail.

"But Wait! My PC Specs Sound Like Hieroglyphics!"

No worries! There are plenty of websites that can decode those cryptic codes for you. Check out system requirements lab or Can You Run It. Just enter the game's name and they'll tell you if your PC is up to the challenge.

FAQ: Become a Detroit: Become Human Guru

How to check my PC specs?

Press the Windows key + R, type "dxdiag" and press enter. A window will pop up with all your system information.

How can I improve my PC's performance?

Close unnecessary programs before running the game, update your graphics drivers, and consider tweaking in-game settings.

How much does Detroit: Become Human cost?

The price can vary depending on the platform, but you can usually find it for around $20-$30.

How long does it take to beat Detroit: Become Human?

The game's length depends on your choices and how much you explore, but it can take anywhere from 10-20 hours to complete.

How do I make all the right choices to save humanity?

Unfortunately, there's no one-size-fits-all answer here. That's the beauty (and frustration) of Detroit: Become Human – your choices matter!

So, there you have it! Now you're armed with the knowledge to see if your PC can become a haven for these sophisticated androids (or a glitchy mess). Just remember, even if your PC can't handle it, there's always the option to watch someone else play and enjoy the story that way. But hey, where's the fun in that?
