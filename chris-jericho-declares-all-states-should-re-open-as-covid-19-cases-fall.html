Chris Jericho Declares All States Should Re-Open As COVID-19 Cases Fall

The United States ordered even more COVID-19 vaccines and cases are going down. Some people are a little more eager than others to re-open completely.

Disco Inferno tweeted out to boast about how well Nevada is doing right now. COVID-19 cases seem to be falling in rapid fashion, so he wants them to re-open now.

Only 245 new cases in Nevada. Seven weeks ago it was 2543. Hospitals empty. Time to open this shit up.

Chris Jericho, who is not political, tweeted out to say that he agrees. Then Madusa chimed in to say: “It ALL NEEDS TO OPEN!”

We will wait out 2021 just as we did 2020. In the meantime, the pro wrestling world found a way to carry on and we can’t wait to see what’s next as everything does begin to really re-open again.
