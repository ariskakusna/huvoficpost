Best Nintendo Switch Bundle Deals 2023: OLED, Mario Kart Bundles

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, The Hollywood Reporter may receive an affiliate commission.

While Nintendo rarely marks down their consoles and games, one of the best times to buy a new Nintendo Switch is during the holiday season. This is because the gaming company releases new bundles that take the financial sting out of getting a console and enticing new gamers by including a few extra goodies at no extra cost.

The Nintendo Switch OLED retails for $350, but right now, you can pick one up in a bundle at Walmart that includes the console, the fighting game Super Smash Bros. Ultimate and a subscription to the Nintendo Switch Online multi-player and cloud gaming platform for the same retail price.

Not a fan of Super Smash Bros. Ultimate? Nintendo has bundles for Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom and Animal Crossing: New Horizons too, which are ideal for gift-giving (or yourself).

If the gift recipient already has a Nintendo Switch, check out the top deals on Xbox and PS5 holiday bundles too.

Ahead, you’ll find all of the Nintendo Switch bundles that are now available for the holidays.

BEST DEAL

Nintendo Switch OLED

Bundled with The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Walmart has the Nintendo Switch OLED: The Legend of the Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom bundle going for $315. It comes the OLED model of the console with a sharper and larger seven-inch display, two gold and green Joy-Con controllers and a Tears of the Kingdom digital copy (a $70 value). With this bundle, you’re saving $105 when you compare it to getting each one by one. In fact, you’re saving $35 off the console itself.

Nintendo

Nintendo Switch OLED

Bundled with Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Walmart has the Nintendo Switch OLED: Super Smash Bros. Ultimate bundle priced at $350. It comes the OLED model of the console with a sharper and larger seven-inch display, two gray Joy-Con controllers, a digital download of the game (a $60 value) and a free three-month membership for Nintendo Switch Online (an $8 value). Altogether, you’re saving $68 when you get this bundle, compared to getting each one individually.

Plus, you can buy it online right now for in-store pickup within one hour (pending local availability).

Nintendo

Nintendo Switch

Bundled with Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

The classic edition of the Nintendo Switch/Mario Kart 8 Deluxe bundle goes for $299 at Walmart. It includes the standard 6.2-inch display version of the console, one blue Joy-Con and one red Joy-Con controllers (two in total), a digital copy of Mario Kart 8 Deluxe (a $60 value) and three months of Nintendo Switch Online (an $8 value) for multi-player and cloud gaming access. You’re saving $68 with this bundle instead of getting the game and online subscription separately.

Depending on availability, the bundle is also ready for one-hour pick up at your local Walmart.

Nintendo

Nintendo Switch Lite: Isabelle’s Aloha Edition

Bundled with Animal Crossing: New Horizons

For something more portable, the Nintendo Switch Lite: Isabelle’s Aloha Edition bundle comes with the “lite” handheld version of the Nintendo Switch in coral, which doesn’t have detachable Joy-Con controllers or compatibility with TVs, and a digital download code for Animal Crossing: New Horizons (a $60 value). Since Target has this Nintendo bundle priced at $200, you’re basically getting the game for free.

Select Pickup at checkout and get the Nintendo Switch Lite within two to six hours at your local Target store, depending on availability. Shipt members can also get delivery within one hour.

Nintendo

Nintendo Switch Lite: Timmy & Tommy’s Aloha Edition

Bundled with Animal Crossing: New Horizons

If coral isn’t your color, Target has the Nintendo Switch Lite: Timmy & Tommy’s Aloha Edition bundle going for $199. It has everything that the above bundle comes with, but the handheld console comes in turquoise instead of coral.

The bundle is also available for in-store pickup at select local Walmart stores.
