13 Things Maggie Holladay, Claude Home Founder, Would Buy Again

1

Augustinus Bader The Cream

Credit: Violet Grey

"I found The Cream through Violet Grey—I'm friends with Cassandra Grey, the founder. She was promoting it like crazy and I trust everything she says, skincare-wise. You don't need much else in your skincare routine other than this cream. I use this and Biologique Recherche toner. "

2

Loewe Small Cushion Canvas & Leather Tote

Credit: Nordstrom

"I walked into the Loewe store and was obsessed with this bag right away. I needed a nice tote bag for work to carry my laptop in, so it felt like a very necessary purchase. But it's also nice that you can dress up for going to dinner. It's also my travel bag. I use it 24/7–I'm waiting for the day it just collapses on me."

3

Byredo Tree House Candle

Credit: Nordstrom

"A friend of mine was wearing a Tree House room fragrance as a perfume one day, and I was obsessed with it. Then I discovered Byredo made this room fragrance as a candle. Now, I have multiples of them in my house. I always like to keep them burning. This scent is a little holiday, but also a masculine, refreshing scent."

4

Chanel Slingbacks Goatskin & Grosgrain

Credit: Chanel

"These were my first Chanel purchase, from years ago, and a shoe that I wear a lot of the time because it's very classic and easily styled. I love heels but I'm tall and not the greatest at walking in them, but these are the perfect height. I usually wear them with black jeans and a t-shirt."

5

Simone Bodmer Turner Valentine Ceramic Vessel

Credit: MatchesFashion

"Simone gifted [my first] to me and it's the perfect piece. I think everyone should have it in their home for flowers. When the light hits it, it's the most stunning piece in the world. I have one in my home in New York, and I just ordered one for my apartment in L.A."

6

Hästens Medium Pillow

Credit: Hästens

"This is honestly just the coziest pillow to put your head in at night. Super plush. I have regular linen sheets from Parachute, and then a giant oversize linen duvet that covers the pillow. It looks very seamless and flattering."

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

Henry Wilson Sculpted Calacatta Marble Lamp

Credit: Claude Home

"I'm absolutely obsessed with this designer and all of his work. His lamps are amazing. The Calacatta is a fun pop of color to have in the home and a very elevated statement piece."

8

Aeyde Black Calf

Now 12% Off

Credit: Aeyde Studio

"These are the perfect weather-getting-colder boot. I always have a fear of wearing boots with dresses and skirts, and this is the only boot I've allowed myself to do that with. It's a fun addition to my wardrobe and also looks great tucked under jeans."

9

The Feel Studio The Black Genuine

Credit: The Feel

"These are the black jeans I've been looking for my entire life. I've had them for three years and they're still in perfect condition. They've worn in with me, but they don't look worn-in."

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Claude Home Willow Marble Bowl

Credit: Claude Home

"We designed this bowl a few months ago and it's the first product we've made [at Claude Home]. For me, I have two in my house right now. I find it to be a great center piece, or even chic on a shelf. It's elevated, but very simple."

11

CB2 Marta Tasting Glasses Set of 8

Credit: CB2

"I started buying these about five years ago. They're super thin and fragile, so they break easily, but they're so affordable that it's not a big deal. I've had them in every apartment I've lived in, in New York, in my office, and now in L.A. I didn't buy a bed yet in L.A. but I bought these cups."

12

Toteme Espera Rib Tank

Credit: Totême

"Just a simple tank top that you can dress up or down. The cut on tank tops is very important to me, and this one is perfect. I'm very much a jeans and t-shirt person, so I tuck it into jeans with either the Chanel slingbacks, or my boots, or even sneakers."

13

Bottega Veneta High-Rise Straight-Leg Jeans

Credit: Net-a-Porter

"I'm not much of a dress girl, so having a few really good trousers or jean options for dressing up is so important. And that's exactly what these jeans are."

Halie LeSavage is the fashion commerce editor at Harper's BAZAAR. Her style reporting covers everything from reviewing the best designer products to profiling emerging brands and designers. Previously, she was the founding retail writer at Morning Brew and a fashion associate at Glamour.
